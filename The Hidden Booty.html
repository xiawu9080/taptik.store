Game Description

The Hidden Booty Tap smart. Track clues. Find the treasure! Set sail on a puzzling pirate adventure in The Hidden Booty, a clever treasure-hunt game where intuition meets strategy. Your goal? Discover the hidden treasure buried beneath one of many mysterious cards — before your moves run out! Tap on cards to reveal directional clues pointing toward the treasure. Each clue brings you closer, but choose wisely — every tap counts! With a limited number of moves, every decision is critical. Can you outwit the map and uncover the legendary Hidden Booty?
