Hide And Seek Friends!

Game Description

The good old classic hide & seek. Play either as a seeker or as a hider and build your shelters from cars or office desks, hide in the water, in the hay pile, in the cornfield, in the boss office and most importantly, push others in the seekers vision field. Try to be kind though. Hide N Seek! : Dive into the ultimate online Hide & Seek experience! Battle in real-time multiplayer matches across immersive, interactive worlds. Play as the cunning hider or relentless seeker. Outsmart rivals, master stealth or track with precision. Non-stop action, strategy, and global fun await!
