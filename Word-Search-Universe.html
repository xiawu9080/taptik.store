Word Search Universe

Game Description

Word Search Universe is a fun and relaxing puzzle game where you search for hidden words in a grid. The game has 20 chapters, and each one comes with easy, medium, and hard levels to match your skill. In every level, you need to find 6 hidden words. If you get stuck, you can use a hint to help you out. Its a great way to have fun, improve your vocabulary, and keep your brain sharp!

How to Play

Follow the in-game instructions to play. Typically uses mouse, keyboard, or touch controls.
