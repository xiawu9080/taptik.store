Sprunki Ketchup Mod

Game Description

Sprunki Ketchup Mod - A game with physics of throws and ragdoll elements. Throw ketchup so as to get into the Sprunki and turn it into a ketchup version! Try your hand at 30 levels! The difficulty will increase, and the maps will increase. Think with your head: calculate the perfect trajectory of the throw and use the bounces to win!
