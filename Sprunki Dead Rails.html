Sprunki Dead Rails

Game Description

Sprunki Dead Rails - protect the train! Cooperative survival against zombies with block building. To make the game easier, you can buy a turret that will help destroy zombies. Play on one of 6 maps. The maps themselves are huge! Play with a friend, or in single player mode. The game has 15 Sprunki skins for both players, as well as 12 different blocks. The game has 4 types of weapons: pistol, PP, AK-47, RPG.

How to Play

Follow the in-game instructions to play. Typically uses mouse, keyboard, or touch controls.
