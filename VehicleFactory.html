VehicleFactory

Game Description

VehicleFactory is a creative physics-based builder game where your engineering skills are put to the test! Construct your own vehicle by attaching components like wheels, special wheels, and ventilators. Once your design is ready, hit the Start button and watch your custom creation navigate a dynamic physics map. Will your build make it to the destination? Web Dev https://www.crazygamesx.com/
