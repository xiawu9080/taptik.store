Game Description

Wicked Whirl Enter the swirling chaos of Halloween in Wicked Whirl, the spookiest match-3 puzzle game of the season! Match haunted items like glowing pumpkins, creepy eyeballs, magic potions, and mischievous skulls to cast spells, break curses, and uncover hidden surprises. With every swipe, youll venture deeper into eerie forests, haunted mansions, and cursed graveyards. Can you survive the whirl of wicked challenges and unlock all the spooky secrets before midnight strikes?
